About us

TRANSPARENT is a Spain-based specialized data intelligence company that builds and maintains data analytics products for institutions seeking a better understanding of the short term rental industry. TRANSPARENT has the largest data set for the market and is pioneer of this type of services for the alternative accommodation market.

Operating since 2016 with offices in Madrid and San Francisco, TRANSPARENT has a deep understanding of the latest market trends and works closely with first class Public Institutions, Property Developers, Property Management Companies, Hotels, and other market players. qq
